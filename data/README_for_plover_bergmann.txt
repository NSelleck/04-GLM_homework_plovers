# Column headings

# ID: Identifier for each unique individual. Some individuals were measured on multiple occasions
# Mass: Body mass measured on capture
# Wing: Wing length measured on capture
# Age: Age class on individual on capture: 1 = HY/SY; 2 = AHY/ASY; 3 = Unknown
# Year: Year of capture 1-16 (2001-2017)
# Month: Month of capture (4 and 13 both correspond to April, with 4 associated with April on the breeding grounds and 13 on the non-breeding grounds)
# Flyway: Grouping factor for the Atlantic (1) and Central (2) flyways
# Season: Grouping factor for breeding (1) and non-breeding (2) captures
# Latitude: Standardized latitude of capture
# Temp: Standardized mean seasonal temperature (1970-2000) at area of capture
# Groups: Flyway X Season grouping factor
